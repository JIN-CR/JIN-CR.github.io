    JIN, Cong-Ren (金淙仁)¶

    I am a recent graduate with a Master's degree in Statistics from the University of Southampton. Throughout my academic journey, I have developed a strong foundation in statistical theory and methodology, as well as experience in data analysis and programming.

    During my Master's programme, I gained knowledge and skills in several areas, including machine learning, statistical inference, and data visualisation. I also completed my dissertation on exploring the performance of two specified mainstream methods in unsupervised learning for sensory linguistics data exploration.

    In addition to my academic pursuits, I have also been developing my programming skills in Python and R. I am particularly interested in the field of Natural Language Processing (NLP) and have been exploring this area through self-study and personal projects.

    As a recent graduate, I am actively seeking opportunities to apply my skills and knowledge in a professional setting. I am interested in pursuing a PhD in NLP or exploring career opportunities in data analysis, statistics, or related fields.
